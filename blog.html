Looking back at an amazing week

June 26th, 2017

Somehow it has taken me this long to update my blog... No matter, I just wanted to say that my week at WWDC was incredible. Not only did I get to visit California and attend an actual Apple keynote, but I also met some great people along the way which made my experience a worthwhile one (shoutout to Isabel, Marc, Julia and Ryan!).

I'm not going to lie. I just typed up a whole blog post about my experience, but it ended up being way too long and all over the place to share. I can't help but feel the collective shame of all my previous writing teachers. To say the least, I created more memories the week of WWDC than I have in a long while. And whenever I feel like I have no motivation, I look at my entry badge and remember what got me there in the first place.

As much as I want to go back next year, I hope others who haven't gone get the chance to experience what I did. I'm sure you'll love it just as much as I did!
